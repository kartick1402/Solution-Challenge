Transgender sexuality

"Transgender" is not a form of sexuality; rather, it is a term related to gender identity. Gender identity refers to an individual's deeply-felt internal experience of their own gender, which may or may not align with the sex assigned to them at birth. Transgender individuals identify with a gender different from the one they were assigned at birth.

Why some women are Transgender

Being transgender is not a choice. It is an aspect of an individual's identity related to their gender. Transgender people experience a gender identity that differs from the sex assigned to them at birth. It's essential to understand that being transgender is not about sexual orientation; it is about one's understanding and expression of their own gender.
